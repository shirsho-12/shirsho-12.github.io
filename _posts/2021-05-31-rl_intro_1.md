According to Wikipedia, __Reinforcement learning (RL) is an area of machine learning concerned with how intelligent agents ought to take actions in an environment in order to maximize the notion of cumulative reward.__[^1]
[^1]: Hu, J.; Niu, H.; Carrasco, J.; Lennox, B.; Arvin, F. (2020). ["Voronoi-Based Multi-Robot Autonomous Exploration in Unknown Environments via Deep Reinforcement Learning"](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/9244647). IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology. 69 (12): 14413-14423.
